Pong

by Codecademy user Codicus Wings

Instructions for single player:

  1. Press the spacebar to start the game
  2. Keys to move up and down:
    • W and S
    • ↑ and ↓
    • A and Z
    • ' (apostrophe) and / (forward slash)
  3. Score 10 points to win. Good luck!

Instructions for double player:

  1. Press the spacebar to start the game
  2. Keys to move up and down:
    • Left player uses A and Z
    • Right player uses ' (apostrophe) and / (forward slash)
  3. Score 10 points to win. Good luck!
